Newsletter

For the past three legislative sessions, the Massachusetts Legislature has allocated $750,000 annually to pilot a Farm to School Grant Program. Now, we need to pass the bill, “An Act establishing the Massachusetts farm to school program” (Bill Fact Sheet: https://www.massfarmtoschool.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/S311-H565-Legislative-Session-Farm-to-School-Bill-Info-Sheet.pdf) to make the program permanent, and we need YOUR support. Please send a letter directly to your Massachusetts legislators encouraging them to advance the Farm to School bill this legislative session — H.565 (https://malegislature.gov/Bills/194/H565/BillHistory) and S.311 (https://malegislature.gov/Bills/194/S311). A message has been drafted, but we encourage you to personalize the email to make your message more powerful — you can type directly into the message field! Share your story and why you believe a farm to school grant program and local food incentive will be good for students, for farmers, and for communities now more than ever! Write your legislators now! → https://mafoodformakids.org/get-involved
