WHAT SIGNING UP MEANS.

Signing up will subscribe you to the MA Food for MA Kids Google Group where you will receive news & action alerts.

There is an option to be engaged in the Coalition and listed publicly as an organizational supporter of our current priority legislation to create a Massachusetts Farm to School Grant Program and Local Food Incentive Program. If you choose to sign as an organization, you will be listed on the ‘Our Coalition’ page.

The coalition meets the first Thursday of each month at 2pm — members receive meeting invitations.
